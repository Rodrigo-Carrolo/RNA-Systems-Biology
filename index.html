Research Areas

Our interdisciplinary research focuses on understanding RNA biology through computational and experimental approaches.

RNA Regulation

We investigate the molecular mechanisms that control gene expression at the RNA level, including post-transcriptional regulation, RNA-protein interactions, and alternative splicing.

Computational Biology

Our team develops and applies computational methods to analyze RNA-seq data, predict RNA structures, and model complex biological networks.

Systems Biology

We study how RNA networks integrate to control cellular functions and investigate how their disruption contributes to disease mechanisms.
